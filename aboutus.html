Jeff Sanderson

Jeff Sanderson grew up in Lodgepole, Nebraska, where he spent his high school summers working for grain elevators, cleaning silos and planting/harvesting wheat. He has 16 years of management experience in the call center industry and nearly 11 years of sales experience with both inside and outside sales. He has worked everything from one on one business interactions to government contract negotiations. Jeff and Nixie married in 2012, and their daughter was born in 2013. They spent two years living in the Texas panhandle working for a local newspaper, and despite a brief relocation in New Mexico, the panhandle is still home. Jeff began web development in 2015, and is skilled in CSS, HTML and JavaScript. He enjoys building custom web designs for a variety of clients. He understands the dedication of rural business owners and specializes in small business web solutions.
